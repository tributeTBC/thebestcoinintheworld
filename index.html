Question: What is the total supply of Tribute tokens?
Answer: The total supply of Tribute tokens is 42 billion, inspired by the famous phrase "The answer to life, the universe, and everything." In a playful homage to this concept, the token supply has been set to 42 billion. The Tribute ecosystem implements a deflationary mechanism through taxes. This means that the tax feature creates deflationary pressure until only 21 billion Tribute tokens are left in circulation. This design ensures the scarcity and value preservation of the remaining tokens.
Question: How does the tax mechanism work?
Answer: Whenever someone sends or receives Tribute tokens, a diminishing tax rate comes into effect. It starts with a total tax fee of 10% and gradually decreases to 0% if you can hold the tokens for 42 hours. This mechanism, known as the "Curse of the Weak Hands," discourages short-term trading and promotes long-term holding. It also helps combat bots, as they would be subject to the maximum tax rate if they attempt to manipulate transactions. If you don't send or receive Tribute tokens for 42 hours, you can make a transaction with no fee applied. It's important to note that every send or receive of Tribute tokens resets this 42-hour timer.
